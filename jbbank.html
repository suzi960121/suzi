Secondary Research

Market Research

There is a growing demand for fintech apps like JB Bank due to the increasing popularity of digital payments and money transfers.

Consumers are seeking user-friendly and secure platforms for managing their financial transactions.

Trend Research

Open banking, or BaaS (Banking as a Service)
A key fintech trend facilitating secure data sharing among financial institutions, tech companies, and users. It enables third-party access to financial data for personalized solutions like budgeting apps and investment platforms. Facebook and Instagram are leading this trend, with others following suit. Open banking has become well-integrated into financial ecosystems.
